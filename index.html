I’m Running Across Texas – For Every Kid Who Needs Hope

Why I Run

I was in the foster system, adopted at 6, and faced serious struggles growing up. Now at 18, I'm running across Texas in the summer of 2026 to raise money for Jonathan’s Place — a safe haven for kids who need protection and love, just like I did.

The Journey

From El Paso to Orange, TX — over 860 miles on foot. I’ll cross the entire state to show what resilience looks like and prove that your past doesn’t define you.
